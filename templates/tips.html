1. Water Properly

Do not overwater. Most indoor plants need watering only when the top soil feels dry.

2. Provide Sunlight

Place plants where they receive appropriate light. Some need direct sunlight, others prefer shade.

3. Use Good Soil

Well-drained soil helps prevent root rot and keeps plants healthy.

4. Clean Leaves

Wipe leaves occasionally to remove dust so plants can absorb sunlight better.

5. Fertilize Occasionally

Use organic fertilizer once every few weeks for better growth.

6. Choose the Right Pot

Make sure pots have drainage holes to avoid water accumulation.
