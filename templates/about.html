Our Nursery

The Flora Frame is an online plant nursery dedicated to bringing nature closer to your home. We offer a wide variety of indoor, outdoor, medicinal, gifting, and decorative plants that are healthy, affordable, and easy to maintain.

Our mission is to promote a greener lifestyle and help people create beautiful and refreshing spaces filled with plants.

About the Owner

This project is developed and managed by Ashlesha Parte, a B.Sc. Computer Science student passionate about technology, design, and sustainable living.

The Flora Frame is created as a web-based project to combine technical skills with a love for nature and creativity.
